## OctoAcme Project Management Overview

OctoAcme follows a structured, phase-based approach to project management that balances planning rigor with the flexibility needed for iterative delivery. Our process is designed to scale institutional knowledge across teams while maintaining clarity, accountability, and customer focus.

### Core Process Phases

**1. Initiation**
- Validate business need and stakeholder alignment
- Create lightweight project plans and define success criteria
- Establish clear ownership with named PM and Product Lead

**2. Planning**
- Break work into manageable increments and sprints
- Identify dependencies, risks, and resource requirements
- Align timelines across related initiatives

**3. Execution**
- Manage day-to-day delivery and sprint cycles
- Track progress against commitments
- Escalate blockers and maintain communication cadence

**4. Release**
- Standardize deployment processes to reduce risk
- Ensure observability and rollback capabilities
- Coordinate cross-functional release activities

**5. Close & Improve**
- Capture lessons learned and team feedback
- Identify improvements for future projects
- Convert insights into process enhancements

### Key Principles

- **Customer-first:** Prioritize customer value and usability in every decision
- **Iterative delivery:** Deliver small, testable increments to enable feedback and course correction
- **Clear ownership:** Each project has named PM and Product Lead with defined decision authority
- **Data-informed:** Measure impact and iterate based on evidence and metrics
- **Psychological safety:** Encourage feedback, learning, and continuous improvement
